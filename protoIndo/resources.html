Linguistics Glossary

A collection of terms related to/helpful for understanding linguistcs.

Cognate
- a word with the same etymological origin as another, typically through a shared parent language.
Language Family
- A group of languages related through descent from a common ancestral language or parental language.
Parent Language
- The language from which another language derives from.
Proto-language
- The common parent language of a language family.
